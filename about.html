Hello! I am Sofia, a computer science graduate with particular interest in Web Development. Although I aspire to become a full stack developer, I am more passionate about front end development. Basically, my love for art has driven this passion for web devlopement. If web development is Art then Text editor is my canvas. Giving life to a simple design is what web developers do. And watching my imagination come live always gives me pleasure. Till now I have worked on various projects with Bootstrap, CSS Flex, CSS Grid, Animation, JavaScript REACT JS for the Front-End and have used PHP, ASP.NET in the Back-End development.

Further, my research interest lies in Data Science, particularly in Deep Learning and Transfer learning. I did my undergraduate thesis on Natural Language Processing where a comparative study was done on two approaches (Traditional & Transfer Learning) towards Sentiment Analysis .

Lastly, as a person I'm very pro-active. I believe in productivity. I criticize myself and my work more than criticizing others and constantly work on self-development. Thus I always tend to stay on the learning curve.
